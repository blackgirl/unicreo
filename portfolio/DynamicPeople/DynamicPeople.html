Dynamic People

What we did

Dynamic People is a website which delivers services of recruitment and career counseling. Dynamic People connects recruiters with job seekers.

Key features:

  • Website consists of blocks for those who are looking for a job and for companies.
  • News feed allows posting the latest news on the home page.
  • There is a possibility to download a CV or create a new one using data from LinkedIn.
  • List of vacancies is connected with third party website.

Technologies used
